# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Global users seeking to read, listen, search, and study the Quran. This includes everyday readers reciting their daily portion, students of classical Arabic, and listeners following recitations. They require a highly accessible, readable, and distraction-free environment.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-fidelity, elegant, and modern interface for the Quran, featuring high-fidelity Mushaf reading layouts, multi-language translation and reciter support, and linguistic root analysis using classical dictionaries (Maqayis al-Lugha, Lane's Lexicon).

## Brand Personality
Elegant, Serene, and Scholarly. Focused on high readability, clean serif and sans typography, and soft, natural sepia and gold accents. The design should evoke peace, clarity, and authority.

## Anti-references
Standard SaaS clichés: side-strip card borders, text gradients, tiny uppercase letter-spaced eyebrows on every section, gratuitous glassmorphism, and low-contrast grey text.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity Over Clutter**: The Quranic text is the centerpiece; UI controls should support the reading flow and disappear when not needed.
- **Academic Authority**: Linguistic root data and classical dictionaries should be presented cleanly, honoring the scholarly nature of the research.
- **Pixel-Perfect Authenticity**: The reading view must maintain exact page, line, and word positions corresponding to the physical Mushaf.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA standard contrast across all themes (light, dark, sepia).
- Full text resizing support without breaking layouts or overlapping.
- Seamless RTL layout and bidirectional text handling.
